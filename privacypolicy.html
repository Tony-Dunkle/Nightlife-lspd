LSPD Internal Portal Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Los Santos Police Department (LSPD) Internal Portal ("the Portal") handles information. This Portal is designed exclusively for internal LSPD personnel use and is not intended for public access or general data collection from external users.

1. Nature of the Portal

The LSPD Internal Portal is an internal tool providing access to resources, forms, and calculators for LSPD personnel. It is designed to facilitate internal operations and documentation.

2. Information We Do Not Collect (Server-Side)

Based on the current implementation of this Portal's front-end code, the LSPD Internal Portal does not currently collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or sensitive data entered into its forms (e.g., Application Forms, Report Generators) to any server-side database or external backend system.

Specifically:

  • Application Forms (forms.html): The code explicitly states that applications submitted through these forms "do not currently submit to anything." This means information entered into HEAT, CID, SWAT, ASU, and K9 application forms is not sent to or stored on a server by this application.
  • Report Generators (report.html, admin.html): Data entered into the Police Report Generator and Administrative Report Generator forms is processed client-side within your web browser to generate PDF documents. This data is not transmitted to or stored on a server by this application.

3. Information Processing (Client-Side)

All data entered into the application and report forms (e.g., names, IDs, incident details, interview responses, charges) is processed locally within your web browser. This processing is temporary and occurs solely for the purpose of generating reports or calculating values (e.g., charge calculations).

  • No Persistent Storage (by the Portal): Unless you explicitly download a generated PDF report or copy text from the Portal, the data you enter is not persistently stored by the Portal itself. Closing your browser or navigating away from the page will typically clear any unsaved information.

4. User Responsibility for Data Handling

Since the Portal processes data client-side, you, as the user, are responsible for the handling and security of any information once it leaves the Portal's immediate browser environment. This includes:

  • Downloaded Reports: If you download a generated PDF report, the responsibility for storing, securing, and sharing that document rests with you.
  • Copied Information: If you copy any text or data from the Portal, you are responsible for how that information is used, stored, or transmitted outside of the Portal.

5. External Links

The Portal contains links to external websites and resources, such as Google Docs for SOPs and Discord channels. These external sites are not governed by this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

6. No Tracking Technologies

The Portal does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies to collect personal information or monitor your activity on the Portal. Standard content delivery networks (CDNs) are used for libraries (e.g., Tailwind CSS, jsPDF, Google Fonts, Font Awesome), which are common web practices and do not involve personal data collection by the Portal owner.

7. Security Measures

While the Portal does not store your data, we utilize standard web security practices (e.g., HTTPS for secure communication where hosted) to protect the integrity of the Portal's content and your connection to it. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Portal's data handling practices, please contact the LSPD Human Resources Department or syntaxsamurai on Discord.
