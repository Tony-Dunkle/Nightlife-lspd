This Website Has Been Taken Down

This action has been taken due to the unfortunate state of the FiveM server, Nightlife RP. The server has devolved into an environment where management's conduct has become unacceptable.

The issues are numerous and severe, including:

  • Systematic abuse of position by members of the management team.
  • Inappropriate monitoring and stalking of players.
  • Silencing and removal of community members who voice dissent or opposing viewpoints.
  • Inconsistent and biased enforcement of rules, where select individuals are permitted to abuse their powers while others face punishment for minor infractions, such as spawning a weapon or healing.

This toxic environment is enabled by an ownership that appears disconnected from the server's daily operations, allowing these issues—perpetrated by their own girlfriend and others in power—to continue without accountability.

For these reasons, this website will no longer support or be affiliated with Nightlife RP.
